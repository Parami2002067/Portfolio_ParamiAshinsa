CareerLens AI

Business Analysis Journey

CareerLens AI is a data-driven career intelligence platform designed to analyze real-world technology job market data and identify the skills, career paths, and industry requirements demanded by employers.

This project focuses on transforming a large-scale job posting dataset into meaningful career insights through data exploration, technology role classification, skill analysis, and market trend identification. The extracted insights will support the development of personalized career recommendations for university students, fresh graduates, and entry-level IT professionals.
