CareerLens AI

Business Analysis Journey

Business Problem

Many university students and fresh graduates struggle to understand the skills required by the technology industry. Academic education provides foundational knowledge, but students often lack visibility into current employer expectations, making it difficult to choose the right learning path.

Project Vision

To bridge the gap between academic education and industry requirements by providing data-driven career guidance and personalized skill recommendations.

Target Users / Stakeholders

Stakeholder Need
University Students Understand career options and required skills
Fresh Graduates Identify skill gaps before applying for jobs
Entry-level IT Professionals Plan career growth

User Journey

🎯

Select Target Career

User selects the desired IT career path they want to explore.

🛠️

Enter Current Skills

User provides their existing technical and professional skills.

📄

Upload Resume

System extracts relevant information from the user's resume.

🤖

CareerLens AI Analysis

AI analyzes career requirements and compares them with the user's profile.

🚀

Personalized Recommendations

The system provides:

  • Career Match Score
  • Skill Gap Analysis
  • Learning Roadmap
  • Alternative Career Paths
